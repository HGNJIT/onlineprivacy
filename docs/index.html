How you are tracked online
Cookies

Have you ever visited a website and it asks you to enable cookies? Cookies are like little crumbs left on a website after you use it. They store little bits of data about the specific user that went on the website. They store data which includes listings you have looked and what articles you like to read about so that they can target specific ads towards you of your interests.

Look Alike Analysis
Smart Marketing

Look Alike Analysis or Modeling is an online marketing method. In this method the interests of a couple people are studied and analysed. Then this data is used to target people of similar interest to that of the people tracked and studied.
