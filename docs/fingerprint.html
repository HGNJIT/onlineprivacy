Browser Fingerprinting
Digital Finger Print

Your digital finger print, is a term used to describe your online activity. It is used to see what sites you visit frequently and what you look at on a daily basis. These include things you like to buy or look at, and what things you read up on. All this information is used for targeting ads on you.
