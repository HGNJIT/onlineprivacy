Online Security
Keeping your information secure online

If you were to drop your wallet, and someone were to find it they would have access to information like your name, address, and credit card information. Just like how you must always keep your wallet near you when you go outside the same applies to your information online. Your computer has a unique IP address. This is like the address of your own home. This is the way most people track you, the safest way to hide this address and browse the web anonymously is through a VPN. A VPN bounces your IP from region to region changing it so it becomes untraceable.
