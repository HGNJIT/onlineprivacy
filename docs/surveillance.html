Surveillance Capitalism
Information = Money

Your information can be gathered online and sold to companies so that they can target ads towards you. Cookies are one way they do this, these are little files that store information like what parts of the site you visit the most, or if its a shopping site, what products you buy most often. This info can be sold to big companies so they can target you with specific ads that you may click on and buy things from.
